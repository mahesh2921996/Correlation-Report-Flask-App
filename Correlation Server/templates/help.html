Correlation

Correlation:Correlation (r) is a standardized measure of linear association between two numerical variables. To find the correlation, divide the covariance by the product of the standard deviations. corr(x, y) = cov(x, y) / S_x * S_y The correlation, does not have units and can be shown always to lie between -1 and +1, -1 <= corr(x, y) <= +1
